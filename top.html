Some common questions
were often asked

What are the health benefits of fresh milk?

It’s rich in calcium, protein, and vitamins that support bone health and overall wellness.

Where does the milk come from?

Our milk is sourced directly from local dairy farms and delivered to you within hours of milking.

How do I know if I need A2 milk?

If you experience digestion issues like bloating or discomfort with regular milk, A2 milk may be gentler on your stomach — but it’s best to try it and see how your body responds.

What is A1 and A2 milk?

A1 milk comes from certain breeds like Holstein and Friesian cows. A2 milk comes from indigenous breeds like Gir, Sahiwal, and some Jersey cows.

Why is A2 milk more expensive?

A2 milk is produced by select breeds in smaller quantities, and farms often follow special care and feeding practices, which increases cost.

What nutrients are found in fresh milk?

Fresh milk is packed with calcium, protein, vitamins, and healthy fats, providing essential nutrients for strong bones, muscles, and overall health.
